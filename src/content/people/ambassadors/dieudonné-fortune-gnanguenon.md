Dieudonné Fortune Gnanguenon is a digital project manager based in Senegal, holding a Master’s degree in Digital Transformation Management. He works with Kaikai, where he leads impact-driven digital projects at the intersection of technology and public good. For over a year, he has led a national air-quality monitoring initiative, deploying more than 40 low-cost AirGradient sensors across Senegal, training youth, raising awareness among students, and engaging in high-level programs such as the African School on Air Pollution and now the OpenAQ Community Ambassador program.
