Chathuranga Lakmal is a Scientist (Environmentalist) focusing on ambient and indoor air quality monitoring and management, with experience in the operation of various air quality monitoring equipment, data analysis, and validation of air quality sensors. He holds a Master’s degree in Analytical Chemistry and a Bachelor’s degree in Physical Science from the University of Colombo. Beyond air quality monitoring and management, he is involved in public awareness programs, air quality research activities, and supports the development of air quality monitoring platforms to provide air quality information to communities.
